NHD waterbody shapefile for the whole lower 48
-The NHD waterbody shapefile was exported from the national seamless gdb (7 or 8 GB) on 2019 Dec 29. 
-NHD seamless database was downloaded from http://www.horizon-systems.com/NHDPlus/V2NationalData.php
-Polygon centroids were calculated in decimal degrees (x=lon, y=lat)
-The waterbody table was exported as csv and includes those xy coords
